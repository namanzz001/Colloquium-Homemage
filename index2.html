Brief about the event:

ISTE Thapar Chapter is back with its most-awaited annual flagship event Colloquium 9.0, filled with mind-bending illusions, daring feats, and mysterious innovations. It is a four-day-long event in which the technophiles will be allowed to unravel the war of coding and algorithms. It will begin with the quiz round, testing the rapidness and efficiency of the participants. Followed by the workshops, speaker sessions will conduct to enhance the knowledge of the tech world. Next up, we present our designing event providing the creative heads a medium to show their creative side, which will also enhance their leadership skills. Finally, the Colloquium will conclude with its flagship event, which includes decrypting the encrypted codes in exchange for exciting goodies and cash prizes.
